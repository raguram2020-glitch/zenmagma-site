🏢 1. Who We Are

ZenMagma (zenmagma.com) is a free online gaming platform operated as a personal/independent project. References to "we", "us", or "our" in this policy refer to ZenMagma and its operator.

Data Controller Contact:
Email: raguram2020@gmail.com
Website: https://zenmagma.com

This Privacy Policy describes our practices for collecting, using, and disclosing information when you use our website. It also describes your choices regarding use, access and correction of your personal information.

📊 2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

a) Automatically Collected Information

  • Log Data: When you visit ZenMagma, our servers and hosting provider may automatically record information such as your IP address, browser type, browser version, the pages you visit, the time and date of your visit, and other diagnostic data.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access ZenMagma, including hardware model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the site — which games you play, how long you play, features you use.

b) Data You Provide

  • Contact Form: If you contact us via our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and message content. This is sent directly to our email via your email client and is not stored on our servers.

c) Locally Stored Data (No Server Transmission)

  • LocalStorage: We use your browser's localStorage to save your in-game progress, XP points, streak, recently played games, and liked games. This data stays on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • LocalStorage keys we use: zm_gs, zm_recent, zm_liked_*, zm_user

d) Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect credit card numbers or financial data
  • We do not collect health information
  • We do not require registration or account creation
  • We do not knowingly collect email addresses unless you contact us directly

⚙️ 3. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the service — including rendering games, tracking gameplay features (XP, streaks), and storing preferences.
  • To improve the platform — understanding which games are popular, which features are used, and how to make ZenMagma better.
  • To communicate with you — responding to enquiries or feedback you send through our contact form.
  • To display relevant advertising — using Google AdSense to serve contextually relevant ads that help keep ZenMagma free. See Section 5.
  • To comply with legal obligations — where required by applicable law.

Legal basis (GDPR): We process data based on our legitimate interests in operating and improving the platform, your consent (for cookies where required), and legal compliance.

🍪 4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data stored on your browser.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function correctly. These cannot be disabled as they are necessary for game functionality.
  • Analytics Cookies: We may use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand visitor behaviour. These cookies collect information in an anonymised form.
  • Advertising Cookies: Google AdSense uses cookies to serve personalised ads based on your interests. See Section 5 for full details.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Games embedded from GameDistribution and other partners may set their own cookies. See Section 6 and 7.

Managing Cookies

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, some parts of ZenMagma may not function properly.

To opt out of Google's use of cookies for advertising, visit: Google Ads Settings or the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

📢 5. Advertising & Google AdSense

ZenMagma uses Google AdSense, a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC, to display advertisements on our website. These ads help us keep ZenMagma free for all users.

Google AdSense uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other sites across the internet. This is known as interest-based advertising.

What this means for you

  • Google may use your browsing behaviour across websites to show you more relevant ads
  • Google may share aggregated, non-personally-identifiable information with advertisers
  • ZenMagma does not have access to or control over cookies set by Google AdSense
  • Google is an independent data controller for ads data — see their Privacy Policy below

Your Choices

  • Google Ads Settings — personalise or opt out of personalised ads
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense Program Policies

⚠️ Note: Google AdSense may not serve personalised ads to users in the European Economic Area (EEA) without valid consent under GDPR. We use the IAB Transparency and Consent Framework where required.

ZenMagma uses the following third-party services that may collect data independently:

  • Google AdSense — Advertising. See Google Privacy Policy.

🎮 7. GameDistribution Embedded Games

Some games on ZenMagma are sourced from GameDistribution (gamedistribution.com), an HTML5 game distribution platform. When you play a GameDistribution game:

  • The game runs inside an iframe loaded from html5.gamedistribution.com
  • GameDistribution may collect data about gameplay, including session data and device info
  • GameDistribution may display ads within games
  • GameDistribution operates as an independent data controller — their Privacy Policy applies to those interactions

ZenMagma passes a referrer URL to GameDistribution when loading games (as required by their SDK). No personally identifiable information is included in this referrer.

👶 8. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

ZenMagma is intended to be family-friendly and welcomes users of all ages, including children under 13. We are committed to protecting children's privacy.

  • We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13
  • We do not require registration or account creation from any user, including children
  • Gameplay progress is stored locally on the child's device only
  • Parents/guardians can clear all locally stored data at any time by clearing browser localStorage

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove that information.

Note regarding advertising: Google AdSense for Child-Directed sites uses non-personalised ads where we have reason to believe the audience includes children. We flag our site accordingly where required.

🇪🇺 9. Your GDPR Rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), European Union (EU) or United Kingdom (UK), you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to Access — You may request a copy of personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification — You may request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Erasure — You may request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to Restrict Processing — You may request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability — You may request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object — You may object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint — You may lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us. We will respond within 30 days. Please note that most data ZenMagma stores is kept locally on your device and can be deleted by you directly through your browser settings.

💡 Quick tip: To delete all ZenMagma data from your browser, open your browser's developer tools, go to Application → Local Storage → clear all items starting with zm_.

⏳ 10. Data Retention

  • LocalStorage data — Kept on your device until you clear it. ZenMagma does not control this.
  • Contact form messages — Retained in our email inbox for up to 2 years, then deleted.
  • Server logs — Typically retained by our hosting provider (Vercel) for up to 30 days.
  • Analytics data — Aggregated, anonymised data may be retained indefinitely for performance analysis.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

ZenMagma is served over HTTPS (encrypted connection) to protect data in transit.
